FitSleepInsights Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how FitSleepInsights ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and discloses your information when you use our website, {{.domain}} (the "Website").

Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you use the Website:

  • FitBit Data: We access your FitBit data through the FitBit API with your authorization. This data may include sleep patterns, activity levels, heart rate, and other data points available through the FitBit API.
  • Account Information: We collect only the ID provided by the Fitbit API. All the account information is stored on the Fitbit servers.

Use of Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To provide you with the Website's features, including the visualization dashboard and the AI-powered chat with Gemini.
  • To analyze your FitBit data to provide you with insights into your sleep and activity patterns.
  • To improve the Website and our services.
  • To send you important information about the Website, including updates, security alerts, and support messages.

Your Data and Gemini

We use Gemini, a large language model from Google, to power the AI-chat feature on the Website. In order for Gemini to chat with you about your data, we send your FitBit data to Gemini. However, your data is anonymized before it is sent to Gemini, and Gemini does not retain any personal information that can identify you.

Data Storage

We store your FitBit data in a secure database. We take reasonable steps to protect your information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. However, no internet transmission or electronic storage is completely secure, so we cannot guarantee the security of your information.

Sharing of Information

We will not share your personal information with any third party without your consent, except in the following cases:

  • To comply with legal or regulatory requirements.
  • To enforce our terms of service.
  • To protect the rights, safety, or property of ourselves or others.

Your Choices

You have the following choices regarding your information:

  • You can revoke your authorization for us to access your FitBit data at any time through the FitBit settings.
  • You can close your account and delete your information from the Website at any time.
